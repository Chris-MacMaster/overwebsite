Below are related sites providing information on hero backgrounds, as well as basic guides to get started.

  • Blizzard's Overwatch 2 page. Blizzard is the company behind the development of Overwatch.
  • Youtube channel Your Overwatch. A source for video guides and news about the game.
  • Emongg's twitch channel. Emongg is a former professional Overwatch player who currently streams the game. Watching him and other streamers is a great way to improve.
