Introduction

Overwatch 2 (originally Overwatch) is first-person shooter released in 2016 that is availble for PC and console. The seting is 60 years into the future of a fictionalized Earth, where a team of former internationally sanctioned heroes comes out of exile to stop a new global catastrophe.

The game is a 5v5 objective based multiplayer shooter, where players play as one of the heroes, each hero utilizing a different weapon and related abilities.

Match outcomes are heavily determined by a combination of teamwork, strategy, and individual skill.
