Sample Heroes

Below are examples of heroes that are more appropriate for new players. These heroes generally have weapons that are easier to aim and simpler abilities.

Soldier 76
Cassidy
Reaper
Mercy
Moira
Reinhardt

Source: Activision Blizzard

Below is a brief description of each hero.

    Damage Heroes, or DPS (Damage per Second). They primarily deal damage.
  • Soldier 76-Uses an automatic rifle, effective at short to medium range. He can also sprint and drop down a healing field for him and his allies.
  • Cassidy-Uses a revolver, effect at short to medium range. Cassidy can roll and throw a short range grenade which sticks to enemies.
  • Reaper-Uses a shotgun at close range. He can turn invulnerable briefly and teleport.
    Healers. They speciaize in healing and buffing their allies or debuffing the enemy.
  • Mercy-Heals or boosts the damage output of targets whith a short range beam. Can also fly towards a targeted ally.
  • Moira-Can damage targets and heal herself at close range with a draining beam. Can then use the drained energy to heal her allies.
    Tanks. They utilize their large amounts of health and defensive abilities to provide space for their team to fight.
  • Reinhardt-Swings a hammer for damage and raises a large shield to defend himself and his allies. He can also dash forward and throw a ranged fire attack from his hammer.
